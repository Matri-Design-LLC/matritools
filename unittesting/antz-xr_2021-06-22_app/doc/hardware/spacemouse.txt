Logitech - 3Dconnexion SpaceMouse family (Pro, Enterprise...)
---------------------------------------------------------------

We work directly with the HID messages and do not require a 
driver.

However, you likely will install the 3Dconnexion driver and 
may find our wiki page useful:

https://github.com/openantz/antz/wiki/3D-Mouse
